==Tripblink

Tripblink (www.tripblink.com) is the fast, visual way to get awesome ideas and information for fun trips anywhere in the world.


![screenshot](https://raw.github.com/realdlee/tripblink/master/app/assets/images/screenshot.jpg)

Right now, Tripblink allows you to filter and sort different countries based on certain criteria.  In the near future, cities, food, and activities will be added.  Other likely features are creating/sharing your trips, more ways to sort/filter (budget, weather, themes), and voting on places.
